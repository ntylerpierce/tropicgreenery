Landscape Design & Installation in Melbourne, FL

Thoughtful design grounded in 44 years of Florida plant knowledge — built to thrive in Brevard County's climate, not just survive it.

Landscapes Designed for the Way Florida Actually Works

Most landscaping problems in Brevard County come from the same place: plants chosen for how they look in a photograph, not how they perform in Florida's sandy soil, heavy summer rainfall, and relentless heat. Tropic Greenery has been solving that problem since 1981 — designing landscapes that are as practical as they are beautiful.

Every project begins with a site evaluation led by Joe Alf, whose credentials in Florida horticulture are exceptional: past president of the Central Florida Palm Society, former caretaker of the Florida Institute of Technology Botanical Garden, and a PBS-featured expert on tropical plant care. Joe doesn't design from a catalog — he designs from four decades of watching what works on the Space Coast.

We source plants from Florida-certified growers and install them according to the specific conditions of your property — sun exposure, drainage patterns, soil composition, proximity to the Indian River Lagoon, and your own maintenance preferences. The result is a landscape that establishes quickly, requires sensible upkeep, and looks better every year rather than declining after the first season.

We handle residential and commercial projects of all sizes, from a single front yard refresh to multi-acre estate installations. Every project includes a design consultation, a detailed plant plan, and post-installation guidance on care and watering schedules.

What's Included

Every landscape design and installation project with Tropic Greenery covers the following — no surprise add-ons, no subcontractors you haven't met.

  • On-site design consultation with Joe Alf or a senior member of the Alf team
  • Custom planting plan with species selections matched to your site's sun, soil, and drainage conditions
  • Florida-certified plant sourcing — no plants grown in non-Florida climates that struggle to adapt
  • Full installation by our in-house crew — no day-labor or subcontracted planting teams
  • Bed preparation, soil amendment, and proper spacing for long-term plant health
  • Mulching of all planting beds to regulate soil temperature and moisture retention
  • Irrigation evaluation and hookup coordination (new system installation available separately)
  • Post-installation walkthrough with care instructions specific to your plant selections
  • Seasonal cleanup and establishment check-in (available as an add-on maintenance service)
  • Coordination with our fertilization team for a first-season nutrition program if needed

Why Tropic Greenery — Not a Company That Started Last Year

Any landscaping company can plant something. What separates Tropic Greenery is the depth of knowledge behind every decision.

We know Florida plant failure modes. After 44 years of installations in Brevard County, we have seen every way a landscape can fail — root rot from improper drainage, pest pressure from plants that don't belong in this climate, die-back from species that can't survive a coastal salt wind. We design around those failure modes before they happen, not after.

Our leader wrote the book on Florida palms. Joe Alf's tenure as president of the Central Florida Palm Society and caretaker of the FIT Botanical Garden isn't a marketing credential — it's a reflection of a career spent studying what grows here and why. When you're choosing specimen palms or tropical trees for a permanent installation, that expertise is the difference between a plant that becomes a landmark and one that gets pulled out in three years.

We're lagoon-conscious by conviction, not just compliance. As an officially designated Lagoon Friendly Lawn Care Contractor, every planting decision we make considers runoff, fertilizer load, and water use relative to the Indian River Lagoon. That means selecting plants with lower irrigation demand, using slow-release and organic amendments wherever possible, and designing drainage that keeps water on your property — not draining pollutants into local waterways.

The same family installs and maintains. Because Tropic Greenery also offers ongoing maintenance and fertilization, we install landscapes with our own future maintenance in mind. That means proper plant spacing, accessible bed layouts, and species selections that won't require constant intervention to look good.
