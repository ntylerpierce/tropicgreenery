Landscaping Services in Melbourne & Brevard County

Everything your outdoor space needs — from the first design sketch to ongoing maintenance — handled by a family team with 44 years of Florida experience.

One Company. Every Service Your Landscape Needs.

Most landscaping companies specialize in one thing. Tropic Greenery was built to handle the full lifecycle of a Florida landscape — because a yard maintained by the same team that designed and installed it is a yard that stays healthy. We've been doing this in Brevard County since 1981, and our approach has always been the same: do the job right, use the right plants for Florida's climate, and treat every property like it's our own.

What We Offer

Landscape Design & Installation

Every great landscape starts with a design that respects Florida's soil, rainfall, and heat — not a template copied from a northern nursery catalog. Joe Alf personally consults on complex residential and commercial projects, drawing on decades of hands-on experience with tropical and subtropical species native to Brevard County. We source plants from Florida-certified growers and install them to perform, not just look good at handoff. Whether you're starting from bare dirt or reworking a tired existing landscape, we'll build something that thrives for years.

Lawn Maintenance

Florida lawns operate on a different calendar than lawns in the rest of the country — the growing season is longer, the pest pressure is higher, and the consequences of skipped maintenance show up faster. Our maintenance programs cover mowing, edging, trimming, blowing, and seasonal adjustments calibrated to how Brevard County turf actually grows. We assign consistent crews to each property so your yard is cared for by people who know its history, not whoever showed up that week. Residential and commercial accounts available with flexible scheduling.

Learn More →

Fertilization & IPM

As a licensed Florida Lawn & Ornamental Pest Control Company, we apply fertilizers and pest treatments under state law — not as a sideline, but as a core service we take seriously. Our Integrated Pest Management approach identifies the root cause of lawn problems before reaching for a chemical solution, which protects your turf, your family, and the Indian River Lagoon from unnecessary runoff. Fertilization schedules are timed to Brevard County's seasonal cycles and adjusted by grass type, sun exposure, and soil condition. The result is a lawn that stays green year-round without the over-application that burns turf and pollutes local waterways.

Learn More →

Land Clearing & Grading

Before any planting can happen, the land itself has to be right — and in Brevard County, that means accounting for Florida's sandy soil, drainage quirks, and wetland setbacks that catch unprepared contractors off guard. Tropic Greenery handles full-scale clearing, grubbing, grading, and excavation for both residential lots and larger commercial sites. Proper grading is the single most important factor in preventing drainage problems that kill plants and damage foundations years after installation. We bring the same attention to sub-surface preparation as we do to the finished landscape above it.

Request a Site Evaluation →

Landscape Lighting

A well-lit landscape does three things: it extends the hours you can enjoy your outdoor space, it improves the security of your property, and it adds measurable curb appeal that translates directly to resale value. We design and install low-voltage LED systems that highlight specimen trees, frame walkways, and wash architectural features without over-lighting or creating the harsh "stadium" effect that amateur installations produce. Every system is designed to work with the specific plants and structures on your property — not applied from a generic fixture catalog. We also service and retrofit existing lighting systems.

Request a Lighting Consultation →

Hardscape & Irrigation

Pavers, retaining walls, walkways, and decorative stone work define the structure of a landscape the same way framing defines a house — get it wrong and everything built on top of it will eventually fail. Our hardscape installations are built to handle Florida's freeze-thaw-free climate while resisting the movement that sandy soil causes in improperly compacted bases. On the irrigation side, we design water-efficient drip and spray systems that meet Brevard County's watering restrictions while keeping turf and beds hydrated through the dry season. New installations, system upgrades, and repair service all available.
