Professional Lawn Maintenance in Melbourne, FL

Reliable, consistent care from a crew that knows your yard — not a different team every week with a clipboard and no context.

Florida Lawn Care Is Its Own Discipline

Brevard County's climate is not forgiving of generic lawn care. St. Augustine, Zoysia, and Bahia grass — the three turf types that dominate Melbourne's residential neighborhoods — each have different mowing heights, fertilization windows, and pest vulnerabilities. A crew that doesn't know the difference will scalp one and underwater another, and you won't know it's wrong until you see the damage.

Tropic Greenery has maintained Brevard County lawns since 1981. Our maintenance crews are trained on Florida turf specifically — not generalist landscapers who handle lawns as a secondary offering. We calibrate mowing height by grass type and season, edge to a consistent line every visit, and adjust our schedule around the realities of Florida's wet season rather than sticking rigidly to a calendar that ignores what's actually growing.

We assign consistent crews to each property. That means the people who show up at your home this month are the same people who were there last month — they know where your irrigation heads are, which corner of the yard drains slowly, and what the beds looked like before. That institutional knowledge is worth more than any equipment upgrade.

We serve residential homeowners and commercial properties across Melbourne, Palm Bay, Viera, Rockledge, and the broader Brevard County area. Contracts are flexible — weekly, bi-weekly, and monthly schedules available depending on season and grass type.

What's Included in Every Visit

Standard maintenance visits cover the full scope of your turf and bed areas — no line-item charges for basics that should be standard.

  • Mowing at the correct height for your grass type — St. Augustine, Zoysia, or Bahia — adjusted seasonally
  • String trimming around all trees, beds, fences, and structures where the mower can't reach
  • Edging along all sidewalks, driveways, and curbs to a clean, consistent line every visit
  • Blowing of all clippings off hard surfaces — driveways, walkways, patios — no mess left behind
  • Bed edging to keep turf from encroaching into mulched planting areas (periodic, as needed)
  • Visual inspection of the lawn for signs of pest activity, disease, or irrigation issues reported back to you
  • Seasonal adjustments to visit frequency — increased during summer growing season, reduced in winter
  • Coordination with our fertilization team if a treatment visit coincides with your maintenance schedule

Why Consistent Crew Assignment Matters

The lawn care industry has a turnover and inconsistency problem. Here's how Tropic Greenery is different.

Familiarity catches problems before they become damage. A crew member who has visited your property twenty times will notice that a section of turf is thinning, that a bed hasn't had adequate moisture, or that a tree is dropping something unusual. A crew member on their first visit to your property is just mowing — they have no baseline to compare against.

We don't subcontract your maintenance. Every Tropic Greenery maintenance crew is employed by Tropic Greenery — not a staffing service or a franchise subcontractor. The people on your property are trained by us, insured by us, and accountable to us. That matters when something goes wrong and someone needs to answer for it.

We communicate when something needs attention. If our crew notices a chinch bug outbreak starting in a corner of your lawn, a broken irrigation head, or a plant that's showing signs of stress, we contact you — we don't wait for you to notice it yourself after the damage is done. This is especially valuable for commercial accounts and homeowners who travel frequently.

We've been showing up since 1981. Companies that enter the lawn care market during a boom and exit when it slows are a recurring pattern in Brevard County. Tropic Greenery has maintained continuous operations for 44 years because we build long-term relationships with clients, not just recurring billing cycles.
