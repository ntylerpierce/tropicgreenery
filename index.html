Four Decades of Florida Expertise

Tropic Greenery was founded in 1981 by Joe Alf, whose depth of knowledge about Florida's plant life is rare even among professionals. Joe has been a featured guest on PBS, served as past president of the Central Florida Palm Society, and spent years as caretaker of the Florida Institute of Technology Botanical Garden — a living laboratory of tropical and subtropical species.

Today, Joe works alongside his sons Ben and Dan and daughter Janie — all lifelong Florida residents who grew up learning the land from the ground up. That generational knowledge is what separates Tropic Greenery from companies that arrived last year with a truck and a mower. We know this soil, this climate, and this community.

As an officially designated Lagoon Friendly Lawn Care Contractor, a licensed Florida Lawn & Ornamental Pest Control Company, and a Keep Brevard Beautiful certified business, we hold ourselves to standards that protect not just your yard — but the Indian River Lagoon we all call home.
